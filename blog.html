Prof. Partha Sarkar Principal Of JISCE

Prof. Partha Sarkar received his B.E. (Electronics) degree from Shivaji University, Kolhapur, Maharashtra in the year 1990, M.E. (Electronics & Telecommunication) from Jadavpur University, Kolkata in the year 1999 & Ph.D in Engineering from Jadavpur University, Kolkata in the year 2008. He did his MBA from UTKAL UNIVERSTY in the year 2014. He started his career in Electronics Industries just after graduation. With an affinity towards teaching, he left industry job and started his teaching career as Lecturer in Orissa Engineering College in the year 1992. Subsequently he became Assistant Professor and Associate Professor in the same college. He became Professor and founder Principal of Nalanda Institute of Technology, Bhubaneswar in the year 2007. Again, in the year July 2008 he joined as Principal of Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management (GITAM), Bhubaneswar.


He was there till 25th March of 2013. Thereafter he joined as Director (Curriculum Development) in BIJU PATNIK UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY (BPUT), Odisha. During his extended tenure of Six years and Two Months he was entrusted with additional charge of Director I/c for College Development Council, Director I/c for Center for Advanced Post Graduate Studies. He was also TEQIP-III Coordinator for the University. He also served as Registrar of the University as additional charge. He finished his extended tenure from the University on 31 st May 2019. He is serving AICTE, NAAC and DST as and when required as experts for different purposes. Presently he is working as Principal of JIS College of Engineering, Kalyani which is NAAC ‘A’ graded and NIRF ranked college. Under his leadership only the college is placed in NIRF ranking in the year 2020 and 2021. His research areas of interest include physics, technology, and characterization of silicon CMOS devices for logic and mixed-signal applications, nanoelectronics, Optical Device and Optical Communication.


He has over 50 publications in these areas in refereed international journals and conference proceedings. Prof. Sarkar is a life member of ISTE (Indian Society for Technical Education) and a Fellow of the Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE). He is Fellow of Institution of Engineers (India). He is also Senior Member of IEEE, USA. He was ex Vice- Chairman IETE Orissa Section and Ex- State Secretary ISTE Orissa Section. He had served as Secretary of IEEE Electron Device Society, Kolkata Chapter for two years and later on served as Chairman, IEEE Electron Devices Society, Bhubaneswar Chapter for several years. He was nominated for Marquis Who’s Who in the World, USA in the year 2009 and his Biography included in the 27 th edition of Who’s Who in the World- 2010.


He had also received The International President’s Award for Ionic Achievement by International Biographical Center, Cambridge, England in the year 2010. He received Shri Jagannath Namaratna Samman 2011 for his contribution in technical education in th eState of Odisha. He had received “Prakruti Bandhu Puraskar” from the Department of Forest & Environment, Govt. Of Odisha, towards Protection of Earth and Environment in the year 2015. He had received number of awards from other organizations also.
